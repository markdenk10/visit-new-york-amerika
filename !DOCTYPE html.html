About New York City

New York City is one of the largest and most famous cities in the United States. It is located on the east coast of America and welcomes around 50 million visitors every year. The city is known for its tall skyscrapers, busy streets, yellow taxis and world-famous attractions. People come from all over the world to enjoy shopping, museums, theatres, parks and exciting nightlife. New York is also an important centre for business, culture and entertainment. There is always something happening, which is why New York is called “The City That Never Sleeps”.


  • Location: East Coast of the USA
  • Visitors: 50 Million per year
  • Time Zone: Eastern Time
  • Population Density: 11,153 people per km²

Things To Do In New York

Central Park

Central Park is one of the most famous parks in the world. Visitors can relax, walk, play sports, enjoy concerts or simply escape from the busy city.

Sands Point Preserve

Sands Point Preserve is a beautiful historic park with forests, gardens and waterfront views. It is perfect for hiking, photography and relaxing walks.

Gateway National Recreation Area

This large recreation area offers beaches, nature and historical sites. Visitors can swim, hike, bike, fish and watch birds.

Adventureland

Adventureland is a family amusement park with exciting rides and attractions. It is especially popular with families and children.
